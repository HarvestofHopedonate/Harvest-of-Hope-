About Us

Harvest of Hope provides nutritious food and essential support to children and families facing food insecurity. We organize food drives and direct distributions in partnership with community centers and nonprofits to ensure safe and equitable access to meals.

Founded by Brandon and Diane Brown, Harvest of Hope operates under the fiscal sponsorship of Fiscal Sponsorship Allies (501(c)(3)), serving communities in New Mexico and beyond.
